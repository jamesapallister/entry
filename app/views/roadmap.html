Features

GOV.UK Notify makes it easy for public sector service teams to send emails, text messages and letters.

You do not need any technical knowledge to:

  • create and send messages
  • edit and format content
  • add personalisation
  • send messages in bulk
  • see when your messages have been delivered
  • collaborate with your team

Find out who can use GOV.UK Notify.

Create an account for free and try it yourself.

Unlimited free emails

  • Send files by email
  • Add email branding
  • Choose a reply-to email address

Send and receive text messages

  • Send thousands of free text messages
  • Receive text messages
  • Send text messages to international numbers
  • Tell recipients who your messages are from

GOV.UK Notify prints and posts your letters for you

  • Choose your postage
  • Send international letters
  • Add letter branding
  • Upload a letter from a PDF

Automatic sending

To send messages automatically, you’ll need a developer to integrate with the GOV.UK Notify API.

For more information, read our API documentation.

Keep your data secure

Find out more about security on GOV.UK Notify.

Performance

GOV.UK Notify commits to:

  • sending 95% of emails and text messages within 10 seconds
  • printing and posting letters by 3pm the next working day

See the latest performance data.

24-hour online support

GOV.UK Notify provides 24/7 support for emergencies.

Contact the GOV.UK Notify team.
